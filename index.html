About LEARNible

At LEARNible, we are driven by a simple, powerful belief: everyone has valuable knowledge to share and the right to learn. We are building a platform where educators can thrive and learners can access quality education without barriers.

Our Vision

"To create a world where knowledge flows freely, where educators can easily share and teach their knowledge and anyone with curiosity can learn without barriers"

Our Mission

We empower knowledge creators to build thriving educational businesses by providing simple, powerful tools for course creation and student engagement. Through our transparent platform and fair revenue-sharing model, we make quality education accessible to learners everywhere while supporting instructors in turning their expertise into sustainable income.

Frequently Asked Questions

üí∞ How does pricing work?

Course pricing is set by instructors. Students pay the listed price to enroll. LEARNIBLE uses Stripe for secure payment processing. Instructors keep 60% of course sales.

üë®‚Äçüè´ How do I become an instructor?

Sign up as an instructor, create your first course with videos and descriptions, submit for review, and once approved, your course goes live!

üíµ How much can I earn?

You keep 60% of every course sale. No monthly fees - we only take a percentage when you make a sale. Set your own course prices and track earnings in real-time.

üí≥ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods through Stripe: Credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and bank transfers in supported regions.

üåç Which currencies are supported?

We support USD, NGN, GBP, EUR, and CAD. Prices are automatically converted to your local currency based on current exchange rates.
